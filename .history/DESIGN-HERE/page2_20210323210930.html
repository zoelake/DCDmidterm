Garden

Logo | Style | Mockup

  • Colour

    Our colour scheme reflects the diverse range of plants within our app, and inspired by the four elements from which plants thrive. The primary use of color in our app is for background gradients, which create a youthful and vibrant theme. Our main text colour is white, and our main logo is comprised of black and white. White is also used for the status bar during the questionnaire.

  • Typography

    Montserrat

    Bebas Neue

    Permanent Marker

    We chose the font “Montserrat” for a clean look. We wanted a font that would be easily readable for the user and a sans-serif font was the easiest choice. The font “Bebas Neue” was picked for its boldness while still being readable. We wanted a typeface that would complement and contrast with “Montserrat”. Our final choice for typography was “Permanent Marker”, which was used in our infographic. We liked the playful side of it and thought it was a good addition to our other choices, as well as to our colour palette.

  • Iconography



    For our iconography, we went with colours that found in nature; for this reason, a large emphasis is placed on green. The rounder shapes also connect well with our logo and emulate organic shapes typically found outdoors. To create contrast, we used white as a color for the fonts and/or the buttons. Rounded borders were also chosen to create the appearance of a button- this familiar design creates a connection to clickability in the users’ mind.

  • Graphics

    For our graphics, we took a simple approach- playful and direct imagery. Our users will be shown images relevant to the text on-screen. The colour green is also heavily emphasized. This ties our graphics to existing elements, and falls into line with our existing colour scheme.
