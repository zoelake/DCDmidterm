Logo

Our logo reflects our mission for this app: growing your own plants at home. We wanted to represent the divinity that is life/nature so we used two hands lifting something to the sky, showing how thankful we are to the Greater Power.


We chose the font “Bebas Neue” to go with our logo because we wanted a simple typeface that wouldn’t take the attention away from the logo icon, but supports it.


We used similar line width and weight for the hands and the leaves growing to represent the connection between the two, since we need to use our hands to plant the plants. To create this logo, we relied on proportion as a design principle.
